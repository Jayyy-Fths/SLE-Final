NJ National Guard Recruiter Ready Checklist

1. Proof of identity:
   - Birth certificate or valid passport
   - Social Security card

2. Education records:
   - High school diploma or GED
   - College transcripts or certificates (if applicable)

3. ASVAB and medical notes:
   - ASVAB score report (if available)
   - Any existing medical documentation or accommodations

4. Personal readiness:
   - Emergency contact information
   - Current employment and availability details

5. Questions to ask a recruiter:
   - What MOS options match my ASVAB score?
   - What training schedule will fit my school/work plan?
   - What benefits and education awards apply to me?

6. Next steps:
   - Schedule a recruiter visit or phone call
   - Prepare to complete MEPS medical and background checks
   - Plan your preferred enlistment timeline
