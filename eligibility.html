REQUIREMENTS

01

Basic Qualifications

You must be between the ages of 17 and 35. You must be a U.S. citizen or permanent resident alien and have a high school diploma or GED.

02

The ASVAB Test

Every recruit takes the Armed Services Vocational Aptitude Battery. This score determines which of our 150+ careers you qualify for.

03

Physical Fitness

You must pass a standard medical physical and meet height/weight requirements to ensure you are ready for Basic Combat Training.
